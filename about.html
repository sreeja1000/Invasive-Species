About This Project

The underlying issue of the spread of various invasive species was the lack of awareness about invasive species and the lack of accessibility to this information. Many people were not interested in the implications these species have in our world and are not educated on the harm that they bring into ecosystems.

Additionally, because of this lack of information, individuals could spread nonnative species to different areas as they are traveling since they do not know how to prevent their spread. First taking direct action to reduce the local emerald ash borer population. I organized the removal of over 40 ash trees in the park that were either infested or highly susceptible to infestation. By eliminating these host trees, I helped slow the spread of the invasive emerald ash borer in the area.
