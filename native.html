Species at the Bulk Farm

Invasive Species

Native Species

American Beech

Provides dense shade, supports soil health, and offers nuts for wildlife like deer, squirrels, and turkeys.

Northern Red Oak

A fast-growing hardwood tree that produces acorns vital for wildlife and improves soil stability.

Chestnut Oak

Grows well on rocky slopes and ridges, offering acorns for deer and other wildlife.

Ash Trees

Known for their straight trunks and shade, ash trees help with erosion control and provide habitat for birds and insects.

American Bald Eagle

The eagle pair at the farm is called the cranbury neck eagles since they are located off cranbury neck road. They built the nest together years ago.
